# Mechanics & Systems

## Overview

The Plagas campaign features enhanced mechanics and power systems that make high-level play significantly more dynamic and powerful than standard 5E.

---

## Core Systems

### [Bankai Rules](bankai-rules.md)
The ultimate expression of your character's power - a personal technique that represents mastery of your abilities.

**Key Features:**
- Unlocked through story moments or automatically at Level 13
- Three progressive levels: Base ‚Üí Ultimate ‚Üí Domain Expansion
- Near-limitless customization options
- Trade-offs balance powerful effects
- 1d8 day cooldown after use

**Quick Reference:**
- **Activation:** Full action + Bonus action
- **Duration:** 1 minute (combat) or up to 10 minutes (utility)
- **Power:** Can override most enemy effects (except legendary, other Bankai, or Domains)

## Combat Mechanics

### Using Bankai Strategically
- **Save for critical moments** - Boss fights, dire situations
- **Coordinate with party** - Maximize impact
- **Consider trade-offs** - Make sure drawbacks won't cripple you
- **Timing is everything** - 1 minute is longer than you think

### Action Economy Mastery (Level 15+)
- **Two bonus actions** - Double the swift actions
- **Two reactions** - Respond to multiple threats
- **Maintain concentration** - While doing more things
- **Complex tactics** - Chain abilities together

### Trade-Off Management
Every powerful ability has a cost:
- Offensive power ‚Üí Defensive vulnerability
- Area effects ‚Üí Self-risk
- Buff ‚Üí Subsequent debuff
- Multiple effects ‚Üí Shorter duration

### Bankai Design Philosophy
- **Theme first** - What represents your character?
- **Synergy** - How does it work with your class?
- **Evolution** - How will it grow to Domain Expansion?
- **Balance** - Accept meaningful trade-offs
