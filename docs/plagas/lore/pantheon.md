# Pantheon of Gods in Plagas

## Overview

The gods worshipped in Plagas reflect the diverse origins of its colonial populations, with deities from across LOR finding followers among the settlers, mercenaries, and indigenous peoples.

## Nature Deities

### Gaia - Goddess of Life and Nature

**Appearance:** All living things

**Influence:** Chiefly worshiped by those who dwell in wild places

**Alignment:** Neutral Good

**Domains:** Nature, Life, Light

**Weapon:** Storms

**Colors:** Green, White

**Offerings:** Pebbles, seeds, dew

**Plane:** The Core (Elysium)

**In Plagas:**
- **Major worship** - Listed as deity of Pol Tarei
- Indigenous Loxodon may worship
- Druids and rangers follow
- Natural fit for jungle continent

---

### Silvanus - God of Wild Nature

**Appearance:** Sylvan forest avatar

**Background:** One of the oldest gods having arisen from the soul of nature itself on the continent. Chose AENARION THE HAWK of AETERNUM as his avatar in 1111AE.

**Influence:** Chiefly worshiped by elves, halflings, gnomes, and humans

**Alignment:** Neutral Good

**Domains:** Nature, Life

**Weapon:** Bow

**Colors:** Green

**Offerings:** Oak leaves or branches

**Plane:** The Emerald Grove (Bytopia)

**In Plagas:**
- **Major worship** - Listed as deity of Pol Tarei
- Elven colonists follow
- Represents untamed wilderness
- Aenarion's status (missing/Viridian Wolf?) adds mystery

---

## Religious Landscape of Plagas

### By Colony

**Pol Tarei:**
- Gaia (official)
- Silvanus (official)
- Mixed pantheon (reality)
- Very tolerant of all faiths

**Fort Runestone:**
- Grungnar (official - corrupted)
- Tiamat (official)
- Evil deities tolerated
- Good deities forbidden

**The Sapphire Tower:**
- Kronus (Magisterium)
- Eideon (practical)
- Xan Yae (some scholars)
- Research over devotion

**Lozenberg:**
- Mixed pantheon
- No official religion
- Every god has followers
- Completely chaotic

**Windrun:**
- None officially
- Warforged don't need gods
- Visitors can worship privately
- Philosophy over religion

### Indigenous Peoples

**Leonin Tribes:**
- Unknown pantheon
- Likely nature-based
- Tribal spirits possible
- Ancestor worship
- Loxodon shamans mediate with divine

**Loxodon Shamans:**
- Definitely have divine connections
- Can cause memory loss (divine power?)
- Central jungle territories sacred
- May worship the Titans directly
- Could have unique pantheon

- Pelor's agriculture vs Silvanus's wilderness

### Order vs Chaos
- Bahamut's law (absent) vs Tiamat's chaos
- Eideon's chaotic neutrality popular with sailors

### Knowledge vs Faith

- Xan Yae's balance approach

## Practical Religion in Plagas

### What Colonists Actually Do
- **Sailors** pray to Eideon before voyages
- **Farmers** offer to Pelor for good harvests
- **Hunters** respect Silvanus and Gaia
- **Mercenaries** pray to war gods when needed
- **Most are not devout** - survival comes first

### Temples and Shrines
- **Pol Tarei:** Small shrines to multiple gods, no major temples
- **Fort Runestone:** Temples to Grungnar and Tiamat
- **Lozenberg:** Makeshift shrines everywhere
- **The Sapphire Tower:** Research facilities, not temples
- **Windrun:** None

### Clerics and Paladins
- Rare in Plagas (frontier land)
- Valuable party members
- Often missionaries or seekers
- Divine magic highly respected
- May have secret agendas from their churches
