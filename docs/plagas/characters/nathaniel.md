## Background

Nathaniel is one of the four new heroes who have arrived in Plagas at the start of 1116 AE. Little is currently known about Nathaniel's background or motivations for coming to this savage land.

Based on demonstrated abilities, Nathaniel is a spellcaster capable of enchantment magic.

## Story Arc

### Act I: Paydirt

Nathaniel arrived in Plagas and quickly became involved in the dangerous mercenary work available in the free city of Pol Tarei.

#### Chapter I - First Steps
- Arrived in Plagas
- Met Ragur Thorne and initially joined his bounty hunting band
- Explored Pol Tarei and gathered information

#### Chapter II - Breaking Away & Quick Thinking
- Was fired by Ragur Thorne after refusing to torture a dwarf
- **Notable action:** When a woman kissed Brandon and her raging ex charged at them, Nathaniel quickly cast Sleep to knock him out cold, preventing violence
- Made contact with criminal elements in the city
- Met Markus Swiftsail

#### Chapter III - Building a Reputation
- Reached Level 2 after one month of training and adventuring
- Helped secure supplies for Grey Skar's Leonin village
- Assisted in the search for a dangerous artifact
- Participated in the profitable red scale fishing expedition
