## Story Arc

### Act I: Paydirt

Brandon arrived in Plagas and quickly became involved in the dangerous mercenary work available in the free city of Pol Tarei.

#### Chapter I - First Steps
- Arrived in Plagas
- Met Ragur Thorne and initially joined his bounty hunting band
- Explored Pol Tarei and gathered information

#### Chapter II - Breaking Away & Street Drama
- Was fired by Ragur Thorne after refusing to torture a dwarf
- **Notable incident:** A woman ran up and kissed Brandon in the street, causing her raging ex to charge at them (Nathaniel put the ex to sleep with magic)
- Made contact with criminal elements in the city
- Met Markus Swiftsail

#### Chapter III - Building a Reputation
- Reached Level 2 after one month of training and adventuring
- Helped secure supplies for Grey Skar's Leonin village
- Assisted in the search for a dangerous artifact
- Participated in the profitable red scale fishing expedition
