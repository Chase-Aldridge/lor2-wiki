# Locations & Colonies

## Overview

The continent of PLAGAS is home to several colonial settlements established by the major powers of LOR, as well as mysterious indigenous territories controlled by the Leonin tribes and Loxodon shamans.

---

## Major Colonies

### [Fort Runestone](fort-runestone.md)
**Power Ranking:** S-Tier
- Population: 25,000
- Duergar/Drow slaving and mining colony from CTHON
- Most powerful military force in Plagas
- Leader: General Korax

### [The Sapphire Tower](the-sapphire-tower.md)
**Power Ranking:** A-Tier
- Population: 5,000
- High Elf/Tiefling magical research colony from GUL'RATH
- Run by the Magisterium
- Highly secretive magical experiments
- Leader: High Magister Telerian

### [Windrun](windrun.md)
**Power Ranking:** A-Tier
- Population: 19,000
- Warforged Imperial outpost from FORTEA
- Strict code of conduct for visitors
- Military expeditions into wilderness
- Leader: Lord Myrros, Supreme Centurion

### [Lozenberg](lozenberg.md)
**Power Ranking:** C-Tier
- Population: 11,000
- Lawless free city founded by GUL'RATH outlaws
- Mixed races and religions
- Most welcoming to travelers but also most dangerous
- Leader: The Sand Sparrow

### [Pol Tarei](pol-tarei.md)
**Power Ranking:** D-Tier
- Population: 8,000
- Independent free city (main campaign location)
- Run by sailors, pirates, hunters, and mercenaries
- Some freed Leonin and Loxodon slaves integrated
- Leader: Markus Swiftsail

## Notable Establishments

### [The Sea Drake Tavern](sea-drake-tavern.md)
- Main tavern in Pol Tarei
- Run by Tyr, an old elf barkeep
- Central gathering place for information and rumors
- The Lark performs "The Ballad of the Rusitauri" here

### The Lusty Mermaid
- Brothel in Pol Tarei
- Frequented by The Octopus each evening
- Connection to thieves' guild activities

### The Sea Empire
- Markus Swiftsail's massive galleon
- Serves as his headquarters
- One of the most impressive ships in Pol Tarei's harbor

## Indigenous Territories

### Red Mane Tribe Lands
- Northern rainforest territories
- Known for savage and ruthless warfare
- Raid coastal colonies every few years
- Leader: Raankor Boneshatter (War leader)

### White Fang Tribe Lands
- Southern jungle territories
- Peerless monster hunters and trappers
- Even more elusive than Red Mane
- Notable hunter: Anar Oathbane
- Former member: Grey Skar (ousted after failed hunt)

### Loxodon Shaman Territories
- Central jungle hills (location uncertain)
- Sacred shamanic class mediates between Leonin tribes
- Expeditions encountering them report memory loss
- Notable figure: The Oakspeaker (elusive high priest)
- Ancient shaman: Prophlos

## Mysterious & Unexplored Locations

### The Za'an Ruins
- Legendary "Forebearer" city lost in the jungle
- Exact location disputed among treasure hunters
- Few survivors from expeditions seeking it

### The Golden City
- Mythical citadel of vast riches
- Watched over by sacred Loxodon and Leonin warriors
- Many scholars dismiss its existence
- Favorite legend told by drunken sailors

### Tortle Sunken City
- Rumored to be up north in Plagas
- Details scarce

### South Island Volcanoes
- Small dormant volcanoes in the South Islands
- Recent earthquake activity reported
- Potential danger or opportunity

## Geographic Features

### The Rainforest
- Dense jungle covering much of the archipelago
- Home to titanic beasts and indigenous peoples
- Countless ruins, tombs, and mysteries hidden within
- Geomantic ley lines rumored to exist

### The Archipelago
- Massive island chain making up Plagas
- Mix of rainforest and desertified regions
- Wooden bridges connect some islands
- Treacherous seas between islands

### Coastal Regions
- Where most colonial settlements are established
- Safer than deep jungle but still dangerous
- Major trade and fishing areas

## Power Rankings Explained

Power ratings denote on a tier list of S, A, B, C, D the combined military, economic, and diplomatic might at the disposal of a given faction.

- **S-Tier:** Dominant power, nearly unmatched
- **A-Tier:** Major power with significant influence
- **B-Tier:** Moderate power
- **C-Tier:** Minor power with limited reach
- **D-Tier:** Weakest organized power
