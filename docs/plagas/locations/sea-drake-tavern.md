## Description

The Sea Drake is the main tavern in Pol Tarei, serving as the heart of the city's social life. Sailors, mercenaries, hunters, and travelers gather here to drink, share stories, and exchange information. It's where the party first learned about the culture and rumors of Plagas.

---

- Experienced and knowledgeable
- Runs the main tavern
