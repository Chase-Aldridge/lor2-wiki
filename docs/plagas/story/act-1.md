# Act I: Paydirt

## Overview

The heroes arrive in the savage land of Plagas and begin making their way as mercenaries in the dangerous free city of Pol Tarei. They navigate criminal underworlds, bounty hunting contracts, and the treacherous politics of a lawless frontier.

---

## CHAPTER I: "A SAVAGE LAND"

**Date:** 1116.1 AE
**Level:** 1

### Meeting Ragur Thorne

The heroes meet **Ragur Thorne**, a bounty hunter from Xonos traveling to PLAGAS to hunt. Thorne is assembling a mercenary band and offers work to the heroes.

Also stepping forward to join Thorne's band:
- **Bron** - Halfling warrior male
- **Esra** - Human rogue female

**Payment:** 7 GP per day in Thorne's employ

### The Target

Thorne's bounty target is a halfling fugitive from Zonos, wanted for murder, theft, and larcery. **Wanted dead or alive.**

Intel suggests the fugitive is in a safe house somewhere in Pol Tarei.

### Learning About Plagas

#### Top Narcotics in PLAGAS
The heroes learn about the drug trade that dominates much of Plagas economy:

1. **Tomb honey** - Unknown effects
2. **Jungle grass** - Unknown effects
3. **Sweet wheat** - Unknown effects
4. **Toad rot** - Unknown effects

#### Main Tavern: The Sea Drake

The party visits the main tavern on Pol Tarei called **The Sea Drake**, where they gather information and rumors.

#### Local Rumors

**From the barkeep at the Sea Drake:**
- The bards of the band "the Crossed Blades" are actually assassins and recently killed the captain of a famous ship

**Other information gathered:**
- Tortle rumored sunken city up north in Plagas
- Some small dormant volcanoes in the South Islands, some earthquakes recently

### Notable NPCs Met

- **Grey Skar** - Oldest Leonin freed slave in Pol Tarei
- **The Lark** - Elven female bard playing the "Ballad of the Rusitauri"

## CHAPTER II: "A SAVAGE LAND"

**Date:** 1116.1 AE
**Level:** 1

### The Docks Investigation

The party heads to the docks to gather intelligence on their target.

**Meet Myrra** - An overly cheery female drow at the docks

### Gurn the Dice Player

**Meet Gurn** - A black-skinned tiefling who offers a dice game

The party loses in dice, but Thal cleverly gets their GP back using mage hand.

### Gathering Intel

The party pays 10 GP to a sailor who provides crucial information:
- Learn about **"The Octopus"** - A drow criminal lord
- The Octopus frequents **"The Lusty Mermaid"** brothel each evening

### Street Chaos

A woman runs up and kisses Brando. Her raging ex charges at them, but Nat puts him to sleep with magic.

### The Interrogation Gone Wrong

Ragur orders the party to torture and interrogate a dwarf believed to work with the halfling fugitive.

**They refuse.**

Ragur demands his payment back and **fires them from his employ.**

### Drug Effects Discovered

Through experimentation or observation, the party learns the effects of local narcotics:

- **Jungle grass:** 1hr, +1 WIS, -1 INT
- **Sweet wheat:** 1hr, +1 CON, -1 CHA
- **Alcohol:** 1hr, +1 STR, -1 WIS, -1 INT

### Meeting The Octopus

The party discovers:
- The halfling mark, **Jorin the Rat**, works for the Octopus
- Ragur also works for the Octopus

The Octopus offers them a kill as a job offer. He turns to his lieutenant **Ranos** (a half-elf), flips a coin and it comes up tails - he ends the 10-minute meeting without elaborating.

### More Rumors and Connections

**Ash** hears a rumor that a Fort Runestone force recently hit an island west of Pol Tarei, slaving for Leonin and killing civilians too.

**Keel** meets **Rakar** at the tavern and trades his heirloom to arrange a meeting with the Leonin community on the north shore of the island.

### Witness to Violence

The party sees a brawl in the streets between Ragur and his men versus some apparent half-orc bounty hunters. **Ragur and his men win.**

### New Employer: Markus Swiftsail

**Meet Markus Swiftsail** - A powerful merchant captain whose headquarters is his massive galleon **"The Sea Empire"**

#### First Mission from Markus
Markus offers the party a job to take out some brigands to the west along the wooden bridges leading to the second port outside the town.

### Meeting the Leonin

Through Rakar's connection, the party meets:
- **Grey Skar** - Former member of White Fang tribe council, had a failed hunt and was ousted
- **Prophlos** - Ancient loxodon shaman

Grey Skar shares his story and requests help getting weapons, supplies, and food for his people. The party agrees to speak to Markus on their behalf.

**"Maktin"** - Referenced as "one who is an outside insider in Leonin culture"

**"Ihn Rahkesh"** - When Grey Skar saw the omen (context unclear)

### Ambush on the Road

On the way back from their mission, **3 aarakocra thieves** ambush the party.

**Ash almost dies** but they manage to win the fight.

They find GP and gems on the bodies.

### The Shadow Hand

The party learns about **The Shadow Hand** - A legendary thieves guild based in Zonos, notorious across all of Lor.

The Octopus claims he escaped from them (though this may be a boast or lie).

### Additional NPCs

- **Tyr** - Old elf barkeep of the Sea Drake
- **Yondelberries** - A choice red tasty berry native to Plagas (discovered during chapter)

## CHAPTER III: "A SAVAGE LAND"

**Date:** 1116.2 AE
**Level:** 2

### Training Montage

**1 month passes** - The heroes train and reach **Level 2**

### Fulfilling Obligations

**90 GP worth of goods** were secured from Markus and delivered to Grey Skar's Leonin village.

The party gathered information about the brigands west of town but didn't find them. They learned the brigands are hustling traders on the roads.

**Debt incurred:** The party owes Markus a mission for his aid to the Leonin.

### The Poisoned Children

Markus tells them about an urgent situation:
- A group of children found some kind of **ball artifact**
