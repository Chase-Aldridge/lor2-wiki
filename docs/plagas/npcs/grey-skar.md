## Description

Grey Skar is the oldest freed Leonin slave in Pol Tarei, a former member of the White Fang tribe's council who was ousted after a failed hunt. Now he leads the small Leonin community on the north shore of Pol Tarei, working to build a life for his people in exile.

---

## Background

### White Fang Tribe History

Grey Skar was once a prominent member of the legendary White Fang tribe:
- **Position:** Council member (high-ranking)
- **Tribe:** White Fang - Southern jungle territories
- **Reputation:** Peerless monster hunters and trappers

### The Fall

**"Grey Skar shares he was White Fang, on the council, had a failed hunt and was ousted..."**

- Experienced a failed hunt (details unknown)
- Lost his position on the council
- Ousted from the tribe entirely
- This suggests White Fang has strict honor codes
- Failure is not tolerated among the elite

### From Slave to Free

- Somehow ended up enslaved (likely by Fort Runestone)
- Eventually freed or escaped
- Made his way to Pol Tarei
- Became the oldest freed Leonin slave in the city
- Now works to help other freed Leonin

Grey Skar leads the Leonin community on the **north shore** of Pol Tarei:

Grey Skar **requested help for weapons, supplies, and food** for his people:
