## Description

Markus Swiftsail is the de facto leader of the independent free city of Pol Tarei, commanding the largest merchant fleet and running operations from his massive galleon "The Sea Empire." A shrewd businessman and capable leader, he balances legitimate trade with the rougher elements of frontier life.

## Relations with the Party

### First Employment
After the party was fired by Ragur Thorne, they approached Markus for work.

#### First Mission
Markus hired the party to take out brigands operating to the west along the wooden bridges leading to the second port outside town.

**Results:**
- Party gathered information about the brigands
- Didn't find them but learned they are hustling traders
- Proved themselves capable

### Support for Leonin Community

The party arranged for Markus to provide aid to Grey Skar's Leonin community on the north shore.

**Markus's Response:**
- Agreed to help with weapons, supplies, and food
- Provided **90 GP worth of goods** to the Leonin village
- Extracted a debt: party owes him a mission in return

**This shows:**
- Markus is willing to help but expects repayment
- He honors his agreements
- Pragmatic approach to indigenous relations
- Can be convinced to support good causes (for a price)

### Current Relationship
- **Employer** - Party works for him

- **Ally** - Relationship is positive
