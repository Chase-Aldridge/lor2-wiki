## Description

Ragur Thorne is a bounty hunter from the lawless continent of Xonos who traveled to Plagas to hunt bounties. He assembled a mercenary band to pursue targets and offered the party their first paid work in Pol Tarei.

---

## First Encounter with Party

### Recruitment (Chapter I, 1116.1 AE)

Ragur Thorne met the heroes at the start of their journey in Plagas and offered them work as mercenaries in his band.

**Others who joined:**
- **Bron** - Halfling warrior male
- **Esra** - Human rogue female
- The four player characters (Dylan, Brandon, Noah, Nathaniel)

**Terms:**
- 7 GP per day in his employ
- Hunt for bounty target in Pol Tarei

---

## The Bounty Target

### Halfling Fugitive
- **Name:** Later revealed to be **Jorin the Rat**
- **Origin:** Wanted fugitive from Xonos
- **Crimes:** Murder, theft, and larceny
- **Reward:** Wanted dead or alive
- **Location:** Intel suggested safe house somewhere in Pol Tarei

---

## The Breaking Point

### The Dwarf Interrogation (Chapter II)

Ragur ordered the party to **torture and interrogate a dwarf** believed to work with the halfling fugitive.

**Party Response:**
- The party **refused** to torture the dwarf
- Stood firm on moral grounds

**Ragur's Response:**
- Demanded his payment back
- **Fired the entire party** from his employ

**This reveals:**
- Ragur uses brutal methods
- No moral qualms about torture
- Quick to cut losses
- Doesn't tolerate insubordination

---

## True Employer Revealed

### Working for The Octopus

The party later discovered that:
- Ragur works for **The Octopus** (drow crime lord)
- The target **Jorin the Rat** also works for The Octopus
- The hunt was an internal criminal matter

**Implications:**
- Ragur is part of the criminal underworld
- Not an independent bounty hunter
- May have been testing the party
- Octopus uses violence to maintain control

---

## Combat Prowess
