# NPCs & Notable Figures

## Overview

The savage land of Plagas is filled with memorable characters, from powerful colony leaders to street-level criminals, from bounty hunters to freed slaves seeking a new life.

---

## Major NPCs (Colony Leaders & Powers)

### Pol Tarei
- [Markus Swiftsail](markus-swiftsail.md) - Leader of Pol Tarei, merchant captain
- [The Octopus](the-octopus.md) - Drow criminal lord, runs the black market
- [Grey Skar](grey-skar.md) - Oldest Leonin freed slave, former White Fang

### Fort Runestone
- **General Korax** - Military commander leading northern operations

### The Sapphire Tower
- **High Magister Telerian** - Leader of magical research colony

### Lozenberg
- **The Sand Sparrow** - Leader of the lawless free city
- **The Baron** - Pirate who attacks fishing vessels

### Windrun
- **Lord Myrros** - Supreme Centurion of the warforged colony

## Sailors & Fishermen

### Jack
- Old sailor friend of Keel
- Took party to fishing spots
- Knows good locations for ruby squid

### Jerry

- Owns magic sea shell (casts Gust of Wind 1/week)
- Recognizes bad omens (black seagull)

### Captain Grug
- Half-orc captain
- Can read thieves' cant
- Friendly rival fisherman

### Grease Uglup
- Goblin rogue
- Grug's companion

## Indigenous Peoples

### Leonin

#### [Grey Skar](grey-skar.md)
- Oldest freed slave in Pol Tarei
- Former White Fang council member
- Ousted after failed hunt
- Community leader on north shore

#### Raankor Boneshatter
- War leader of Red Mane tribe
- Leading warbands in the north
- Ambushes Fort Runestone forces
- Scalps enemies

#### Anar Oathbane
- Famed hunter of White Fang tribe
- Moving toward coast with hunting parties
- Contesting colonial expansion

#### Rakar
- Contact in Pol Tarei
- Helped party meet Leonin community
- Keel traded heirloom to him

### Loxodon

#### [Prophlos](prophlos.md)
- Ancient loxodon shaman
- Connected to Grey Skar's community
- Represents the mysterious shamanic tradition

#### The Oakspeaker
- Elusive loxodon shaman
- High priest of shamanic orders
- Never seen outside jungle
- Causes memory loss in those who encounter them

## Mysterious & Legendary Figures

### The Crossed Blades
- Band of performers
- Rumored to actually be assassins
- Allegedly killed a famous ship captain
- True nature uncertain

### The Shadow Hand
- Not individuals but an organization
- Legendary thieves guild based in Zonos
- Notorious across all of Lor
- The Octopus claims to have escaped them

## NPC Categories

### By Faction
- **Pol Tarei Authorities:** Markus Swiftsail, Drôn, Tyr
- **Pol Tarei Criminals:** The Octopus, Ranos, Jorin the Rat, Gurn
- **Indigenous Leaders:** Grey Skar, Raankor Boneshatter, Anar Oathbane, The Oakspeaker, Prophlos
- **Colony Leaders:** General Korax, High Magister Telerian, The Sand Sparrow, Lord Myrros
- **Mercenaries:** Ragur Thorne, Bron, Esra
- **Sailors:** Jack, Jerry, Captain Grug, Grease Uglup
- **Performers:** The Lark, The Crossed Blades
- **Merchants:** Gray Tiefling merchant, Myrra

### By Relationship to Party
- **Employers:** Markus Swiftsail, Ragur Thorne (former)
- **Allies:** Grey Skar, Prophlos, Jack, Rakar
- **Neutral:** The Octopus, Tyr, The Lark, Captain Grug
- **Antagonists:** Ragur Thorne, The Baron, Gurn (initially)
- **Unknown:** Most colony leaders, indigenous leaders

### By Power Level
- **Major Powers:** Colony leaders, indigenous war leaders
- **Significant NPCs:** Markus Swiftsail, The Octopus, Grey Skar
- **Supporting NPCs:** Lieutenants, captains, shamans
- **Minor NPCs:** Bartenders, sailors, merchants, street urchins
