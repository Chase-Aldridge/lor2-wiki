# Aeternum

## The Continent

Our adventurers find themselves embroiled in the drama of one major continent where they were born in the world of LOR: **AETERNUM**. Recorded history here begins over 1,000 years ago (0AE), founded with the first elves and dwarves. The current year is **1111AE**.

The people of AETERNUM have **not had contact with any other continents of LOR for over 300 years**. Magical mists encircle the continent, impenetrable from within or without. The mist is unnatural, created for unknown reasons - whether to protect or to trap remains one of the great mysteries.

## The Four Great Houses

The land is ruled by the intrigues, wars, treaties, and pacts between **4 major houses**:

- **[House Aetherius](/docs/aeternum/factions/house-aetherius)** - Arcane Magic, A Tier
  - Strongest militarily, masters of arcane magic
  - Leader: Lord Apelion (Grand Councilor)

- **[House Meria](/docs/aeternum/factions/house-meria)** - Trade & Commerce, C Tier (FALLEN)
  - Coastal trading power, guild-ruled merchant republic
  - Leader: Lord Oreon (fled to islands)

- **[House Dargo](/docs/aeternum/factions/house-dargo)** - Military & Religion, B Tier
  - Military and religious center, Dragonspear dynasty
  - Leader: Contested (succession crisis)

- **[House Nejune](/docs/aeternum/factions/house-nejune)** - Dark Magic, A Tier
  - Masters of necromancy, source of the Blightrot
  - Leader: Magus Gor (Avatar of Heronimus)

## The End Times Prophecy

Multiple prophecies suggest Aeternum faces either **cataclysm or ascension**. When all avatars of the gods walk Aeternum, the day will come of either destruction or the continent ascending into its own plane. The mist is connected to this fate.
