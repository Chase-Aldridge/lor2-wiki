# Characters of Aeternum

## The Fellowship of the Blade

The main heroes of the campaign, also known as "The Rúsitauri" (The Silver Guard) or "The Sons of the Forest."

### Active Members

#### [Shorea the Liquid Swordsman](/docs/aeternum/characters/sho)
**Silver Dragonborn Samurai | Champion of Bahamut**

Born in Draconiss, trained by Master Waja, seeking vengeance for his destroyed village. Wields the purified liquid dai-katana REN (formerly the cursed Glaw). Achieved Bankai at the Temple of Bahamut. Working to reunite the scattered dragonborn people and rebuild his homeland.

**Current Status:** Champion of Bahamut, Bankai achieved, Level 15

---

#### [Zadshen Dragonspear](/docs/aeternum/characters/zadshen)
**Aasimar Paladin | Oath of Vengeance | Contested King of Dargo**

Born under mysterious circumstances with celestial heritage, connected to the Dragonspear bloodline. Quests to free the three Fallen Angels bound with demons. Made "The King Wish" using the Luck Blade, claiming the throne of Dargo but losing access to the Seven Heavenly Planes. Seeking to become Champion of Leviathan.

**Current Status:** Banned from Heaven, contested claimant to Dargo throne, Level 15+

---

#### [Ulrick Blaze](/docs/aeternum/characters/ulrick)
**Human Paladin/Warlock | Oath of Ancients | Champion of Oberon**

Born during a lightning storm in the Fellwood. Sworn to Silvanus but made pact with Oberon, King of the Feywild. Seeks revenge on Lord Tartulien for murdering his family. Physically transformed by wild magic (aged 18+ years, white hair, scarred). Bridge between the Feywild and Fellwood forces. Can transform into ArchFey form.

**Current Status:** Champion of Oberon, greatly aged and transformed, Level 15+

---

#### [Arca Dawnbringer](/docs/aeternum/characters/arca)
**Human Cleric | Life Domain | Champion of Alaya**

Silent servant of Alaya who has taken a sacred Vow of Silence. Never speaks, communicates through writing. Successfully called upon Divine Intervention three times (resurrected Ulrick, revived Ki and Kai, summoned Angel of Truth). Now bodyguard to Mistraya, the Avatar of Alaya.

**Current Status:** Champion of Alaya, sworn bodyguard to Mistraya, Level 14+

---

### Missing Members

#### [Nynora](/docs/aeternum/characters/nynora)
**Wood Elf Rogue | Mastermind | MISSING IN ACTION**

Born in Meria, grew up sailing with her family who mysteriously disappeared at sea after dealings with 'W' (member of The Guild). Deciphered the warning about Nejune's Blood Moon attack on Dargo. Left the fellowship in the Fellwood (Chapter IV) to pursue her mentor. Last seen in early 1111.2AE.

**Current Status:** MISSING since 1111.2AE, fate unknown

---

## Major Allies

### The Blessed of Alaya

#### Mistraya
**Ancient Moon Elf | Oracle | Avatar of Alaya**

Potentially immortal, oldest elf on Aeternum. Leader of the Blessed of Alaya at Alaya's Rest. Trained Arca and commands the moon elf defenders. Achieved Avatar status during Blood Moon ritual. Currently in magical duel with Magus Gor over the wind of life vs. wind of death.

**Current Status:** Avatar of Alaya, defending Alaya's Rest

#### Eldratha
**Moon Elf | Oracle | Champion of Alaya**

Mistraya's protégé and fierce defender. Escaped Boreas the Ox's assassination attempt with the fellowship. Became Champion of Alaya alongside Arca. Now serves as Mistraya's other bodyguard.

**Current Status:** Champion of Alaya, co-bodyguard with Arca

### The Fellwood Watch

#### Aenarion
**Moon Elf | Commander | Avatar of Silvanus**

Leader of the Fellwood Watch and master of the eagle riders. Achieved Avatar of Silvanus during Blood Moon ritual with help from Mistraya and Fayrus. Defends the Fellwood from Nejune's undead invasion and the Red Horde's northern advance.

**Current Status:** Avatar of Silvanus, leading desperate defense

#### Fayrus the Green
**Ancient Druid | Potentially Immortal**

Elder druid who, along with Mistraya, is one of the oldest elves alive. Leads vanguard of hunters against hobgoblin invaders. Accompanied Ulrick to the Feywild for his audience with Oberon. Instrumental in Aenarion achieving avatar status.

**Current Status:** Active in Fellwood defense

#### Pherien
**Moon Elf | Guard Captain**

Captain of Mistraya's personal guard. Led her caravan to the Council of the Wood. Survived Boreas the Ox's assassination ambush. Loyal defender of the Blessed.

**Current Status:** Defending Alaya's Rest

### House Dargo

#### High Templar Beren Dragonspear
**Human | King of Dargo**

Current ruler holding both the crown and military command (High Templar). Father of traitor Fagren and fallen bastard Geren. Calls Zadshen "usurper" after the King Wish. Led forces at Tower of Dawn before its corruption. Witnessed horrors beyond imagination there.

**Current Status:** King (contested), affected by Tower of Dawn madness

#### Ser Lora
**Knight | Steward of Dargo**

Current regent and steward commanding Dargo in Beren's absence. Led the Dargo fleet. Trusted the fellowship with investigating the Blood Moon conspiracy. Given sealed writ by Beren granting full authority. Instrumental in foiling the Owl, Toad, and Rat plot.

**Current Status:** Steward, commanding Dargo defenses

#### High Priest Ceraph
**Priest of Lokhir**

High Priest of the Temple of Lokhir in Dargo. Performed grand divination ritual with Arca lasting 1 day and night. Teleported to Tower of Dawn to get Beren's writ. Led ceremony during Blood Moon to channel people's emotion.

**Current Status:** High Priest, supporting Dargo leadership

#### Lady Mera
**Noble | Monster Hunter**

Dargo noble leading monster hunting party. Survived white dragon encounter that killed most of her team. Romanced by Ulrick (successfully) and Zadshen (awkwardly). Returned to Dargo to support war effort.

**Current Status:** In Dargo, supporting defense

#### Captain Gaul
**Praetorian Guard Commander**

Commander of Ser Lora's personal praetorian guard. Bears the "silver wings" emblem. Led strike team to capture traitor Prince Fagren. Brought to near death in that battle. Loyal to "climbing silver" faction.

**Current Status:** Recovering, loyal to Lora

#### Baron Chadmerius
**Noble Knight | "The Stout"**

Described as "stout & jacked knight" among the 12 noble houses. Accompanied fellowship into tombs beneath Carth's manor. Lost one eye in battle with the Wardens in the undercity. Brave and loyal warrior.

**Current Status:** Wounded but alive, one-eyed

#### Earl Carth
**Spymaster | "The Sickly"**

Earl of Dargo and spymaster. Approximately 30% of his agents were actually Lathian's vampire thralls. His butler Lathian dominated his mind for years. After Lathian's death, reported "a shadow has lifted from his mind." Now working to rebuild intelligence network.

**Current Status:** Freed from domination, rebuilding spy network

#### Count Perellion Sturnstar
**Mage Noble | Father of Amaud**

Wielder of staff of the magi. Father of Amaud who was murdered by Yon'Tal. Led teleport assault on Warden lair. Performed enhanced timestop in battle. Lost most of his mages in the Warden battle (killed by Demon of Rage taking over Shorea).

**Current Status:** Grieving, supporting Dargo

### The Silver Sentinels (Broken)

#### Zhang Li
**Monk | "The Chad"**

Leader of the Silver Sentinels, martial arts master. Made Heavenly Pact with Lokhir, halving his lifespan for supernatural strength to avenge Amaud. Claps arms with heroes "like a Chad." Removed white robe and wrapped Amaud's body, returning to Dargo in grief.

**Current Status:** Returned to Dargo, half-lifespan, Sentinels broken

#### Christof
**Rogue/Thief | Greaser**

Thief member of Silver Sentinels. Magical whistle creates wall of silence. Blew all the group's grease on bribes. Devastated by loss of Amaud and Kristof Farlington (his brother?). Gave fellowship sending stone. Works now with The Collector forming new underworld alliance.

**Current Status:** In Dargo, rebuilding underworld with Collector

#### Elena
**Bard**

Bard of the Silver Sentinels, lover of Amaud. Sang mythic song about avatars and Aeternum's fate. After Amaud's murder, used cursed red bowl to summon demon. Forced demon to reveal Yon'Tal's location, then vanished through gate to elemental plane of fire seeking revenge.

**Current Status:** MISSING, presumed hunting Yon'Tal in planes

#### Caladenn
**Member** (Role unclear)

Member of Silver Sentinels, introduced them to the Fence in Nejune underworld.

**Current Status:** Unknown after Sentinels broke

#### Amaud
**Wizard | DECEASED**

Lesser noble of Dargo, wizard of the Sentinels. Captured by Yon'Tal and tortured for intelligence about Dargo noble houses. Throat slit by Yon'Tal's Dagger of Heronimus in front of fellowship and his companions. Soul banished, only Wish can bring him back.

**Current Status:** DEAD, soul banished

#### Kristof Farlington
**DECEASED**

Member of the Silver Sentinels who tragically died during the "Bean 9/11" attack on Nejune Tower caused by the fellowship's magical beans spawning monsters throughout the city.

**Current Status:** DEAD, casualty of bean terrorism

### House Aetherius

#### Lord Apelion
**Mage Noble | Grand Councilor**

Sole surviving noble after the Arken Company assassination of all other Aetherius lords. Invoked emergency wartime powers, abolishing the noble council from reforming. Now rules Aetherius with absolute authority.

**Current Status:** Dictator of Aetherius, wartime emergency powers

#### High Commander Taris
**Military Leader**

Commands the Aetherius legion. Led forces at Tower of Dawn before its corruption. Blamed by Beren for the fall (Beren's mind is unstable). Warned fellowship that Aetherius politics might not allow them entry. Gave fellowship his signed seal.

**Current Status:** Commands Aetherius forces, strategic retreat from Meria

### House Meria (Fallen)

#### Lord Oreon
**Guild Master**

Leader of the Meria guilds and de facto ruler. Forbade Brornor from offering non-military contracts. No meetings granted except to highest ranking mercenaries. Status unknown after fall of Meria.

**Current Status:** UNKNOWN, presumed dead or fled

#### Brornor Battlehand
**Dwarf | Innkeeper**

Venerable dwarven barkeep of The Laughing Dragon. First quest giver to the fellowship. Has troubled past with Brax Bjornson. Forbidden by guild lords from offering contracts except military ones. Fate unknown after Meria fell.

**Current Status:** UNKNOWN after Meria fell

#### Gromwinston the Garrulous
**Gnome Bard**

Famous gnome bard resident at The Laughing Dragon. The fellowship generously tips him. Represented Meria at the Council of the Wood, reporting on the refugee crisis and economic situation.

**Current Status:** UNKNOWN after Meria fell

### Dwarves

#### Brax Bjornson
**Dwarf Merchant | Captain**

Captain of trade caravan from Thane's Hold to Meria. Offered Zadshen lucrative contract (11k GP/year) to be sworn guards if dwarves seal their gates. Has personal seal of Thane's Merchant Guild. Troubled past with Brornor. Offered intro to guild embassy.

**Current Status:** Unknown after Thane's Hold fell

#### Lionel Blackpebble
**Dwarf Emissary**

Represented Thane's Hold at Council of the Wood. Pleaded for help to lift siege. Scoffed at northern houses' inaction. Honored fellowship's bravery at the council. Fate unknown after the hold fell.

**Current Status:** Unknown, possibly with Thanor in Underdark

#### High King Thanor
**Dwarf King**

King of Thane's Hold. Led defense that failed when both upper gates (to Gray Wastes) and lower gates (to Underdark) were breached. Now leads approximately 30% surviving remnant in fighting retreat through the Underdark, pursued by Red Horde raiding teams.

**Current Status:** ALIVE, leading exodus in Underdark

#### Wotann
**Dwarf Beastmaster**

Beastmaster at Oakenleaf village. Helped Zadshen perform ritual to transform Find Steed into summoning celestial dire wolf instead of horse.

**Current Status:** Evacuated with Oakenleaf

### Mentors & Teachers

#### Master Waja
**Silver Dragonborn | Samurai Master**

Shorea's teacher and master of Bushido. Trained him in the way of the samurai and the liquid sword technique. His spirit appears to Shorea in visions and critical moments, speaking for Bahamut. Currently with The Lone Drakes ronin band as their leader, revealed survivors of Draconiss hidden in caves.

**Current Status:** ALIVE, leading Lone Drakes

#### Thalindra
**Druid | DECEASED**

Ulrick's mentor and mother figure from Oakenleaf village. Taught him about balance between nature and civilization. Contracted the magical Blightrot disease. Performed all-night vigil ritual with Ulrick, enhancing his lightning pendant. Died peacefully after passing her magic, items, and destiny to him.

**Current Status:** DEAD, died of Blightrot

#### Seren
**High Templar | DECEASED**

Zadshen's first surrogate father. High Templar before Beren. Circumstances of death unknown but shaped Zadshen's early life.

**Current Status:** DEAD

#### Cicero Windblade
**Assassin Leader**

Zadshen's later guardian and mentor. Leader of the Arken Company. Led assassination of all Aetherius noble lords except Apelion, sending the city into political turmoil. Motive for the hit unclear - who hired him?

**Current Status:** At large, successful hit completed

### Dragonborn

#### Gaejen
**Red Dragonborn Elder**

Elder red dragonborn freed from Nejune slavery by fellowship. Knew Shorea's parents. Now helping rebuild Draconiss under Shorea's leadership.

**Current Status:** At Draconiss, rebuilding

#### Ki and Kai
**Red Dragonborn Twins**

Young red dragonborn twins freed from slavery. Tried to emulate Shorea in naval battle, failed jumping and had to swim. Killed by Golden Order ninja Hiren, but resurrected by Arca through Divine Intervention. Now training under Shorea.

**Current Status:** ALIVE (resurrected), at Draconiss

#### Talren and Berai
**Blue Dragonborn Merchants**

Blue-scaled dragonborn merchants freed from slavery. Now helping rebuild dragonborn community.

**Current Status:** At Draconiss, rebuilding

#### Yurio Zarashi
**Dragonborn Samurai**

Shorea's elder senpai. Last sighted in Dargo seeking to duel the knights there. Status unknown.

**Current Status:** Unknown location

#### Bengror and Kishimoto
**Dragonborn Samurai**

Waja's top two students in The Lone Drakes.

**Current Status:** With Waja and the Lone Drakes

### The Underworld

#### The Collector
**Crime Lord**

Black market trader in Dargo's underworld. Traded Fireborne to Ulrick for Paindrinker and gold. Sold Zadshen the Helm of the Ironbreaker. Now forming alliance with Christof to fill power vacuum after Wardens' collapse.

**Current Status:** Rising power in Dargo underworld

#### The Fence
**Elf | Black Market**

Mysterious elf in Meria trade quarter. Contact of Brornor. Helped fellowship sell loot. Also appeared as Fence in Nejune underworld with different operations.

**Current Status:** Unknown

#### Braun
**Rogue Leader**

Leader of rogue group in Gilded Dragon in Dargo. Introduced fellowship to The Collector.

**Current Status:** Active in Dargo

### Other Allies

#### Ferin
**Elf Ranger**

Sole survivor of second mercenary party sent to clear goblin caves. Tortured for a week. Rescued by fellowship. Helped them find suitable campsites on journey back to Meria.

**Current Status:** Unknown, possibly at Fellwood Watch

#### Vonda
**Tiefling Druid**

Sent by Fayrus the Green to study the magical Blightrot affecting the forest. Met fellowship on road to Oakenleaf. Traded items and shared herbal lore.

**Current Status:** Unknown, studying Blightrot

#### Beruus
**Half-Orc Chief**

Wizened half-orc chief of Oakenleaf village. Initially stubborn about evacuating but convinced by Ulrick. Successfully led exodus to Fellwood Watch.

**Current Status:** At Fellwood Watch with refugees

#### Archivist Belvas
**Archivist**

Meria great library archivist. Bonded with Nynora and helped her decipher the Draconic coded message from 'W' warning of Nejune's Blood Moon attack.

**Current Status:** Unknown after Meria fell

## Major Enemies

### The Avatars of Evil

#### Ugluk the Red
**Orc Warlord | Avatar of Bel'Mog**

First to achieve demigod status, becoming Avatar of Bel'Mog (God of War and Orcs). Leader of the Red Horde. Personally broke the gates of Meria with his supernatural strength. Commands massive armies of orcs, goblins, hobgoblins, trolls, and giants. Wields power that explains his army's unstoppable victories.

**Current Status:** Avatar of Bel'Mog, occupying Meria, planning next advance

#### Magus Gor
**Arch-Necromancer | Avatar of Heronimus**

Ruler of Nejune. Became Avatar of Heronimus (God of Death) during Blood Moon ritual through sacrifice of thousands of slaves. Summoned massive undead horde and awakened grave titan generals. Declared war on all realms under "dark power thesis" (only magical elite should rule). In magical duel with Mistraya over wind of death vs. wind of life.

**Current Status:** Avatar of Heronimus, invading Fellwood with undead armies

### The Golden Order

#### Edora the Red Fist
**Red Dragonborn Monk | DECEASED**

Master of the Golden Order's Isle of the Dragon. Bankaid instantly and laid out Shorea with 6-piece combo. Called dragons with "DRACARYS!" command. Died whispering "Hail Tiamat" on Shorea's blade.

**Current Status:** DEAD, slain at Temple of Bahamut

#### Hiren Gajumara
**Black Dragonborn Ninja | DECEASED**

Black dragon ninja captain. Led naval assault on fellowship's ship. Unleashed Shikai incantation of twin cursed katanas Shiren and Shouzen. Killed many sailors and freed dragonborn (Ki, Kai, John Smith). Slain by combined efforts of fellowship.

**Current Status:** DEAD, 15k bounty collected

#### Jato
**Golden Order Member**

Member of the Order reportedly on mission in Meria during Temple of Bahamut assault. Status after Meria fell unknown.

**Current Status:** Unknown

### House Nejune

#### Yon'Tal the Despoiler
**Mage Noble**

Right hand of Magus Gor. Wielder of one of three legendary Daggers of Heronimus (banishes souls). Murdered Amaud in front of fellowship. Had Nebulos as his enforcer. Villa destroyed by fellowship, barely escaped through gate to elemental plane of fire. Being hunted by Elena.

**Current Status:** Fleeing in elemental plane of fire, hunted by Elena

#### Nebulos
**Mage | "The Scarred"**

Yon'Tal's enforcer with long black scar. Target of Ulrick's mission from Oberon. Operations disgraced after villa destruction. Fled to Meria on ship. Mission considered complete by Oberon as Nebulos now hunted by other Nejune mage lords.

**Current Status:** Fled to Meria (now occupied), fate unknown

#### Lord Neerius
**Ancient Vampire | DECEASED**

Released from second sealed gate in Nejune catacombs (possibly by The Circle of the Moon). Killed by devastating critting blow from Shorea in moment of absolute cinema. Dominated Arca and Sho with gaze. Summoned vargheists that killed Zadshen (later revived).

**Current Status:** DEAD, soul destroyed by Shorea

#### Lathian
**Vampire | DECEASED**

Tall butler to Earl Carth. Actually ancient vampire who infiltrated Dargo 100 years ago after Dargo knights slew his family in Nejune. Sacrificed everything to work his way into position. Dominated Carth's mind for years. Controlled approximately 30% of Carth's spy network. Wielded death knight. Bit and cursed Zadshen. Slain in tombs beneath Carth's manor.

**Current Status:** DEAD, fully slain in regeneration

#### Grey Scar
**Greater Doppelganger | DECEASED**

Leader of The Wardens in Dargo underworld. Actually greater doppelganger from the Outer Realm. Building 80% complete gateway to bring more aberrations. Slain as portal collapsed, severed webbed arm witnessed coming through before closure.

**Current Status:** DEAD, portal destroyed

#### Maldrak the Blackened
**Demilich | PHYLACTERY DESTROYED**

Cursed the Tomb of Saint Bel'raa. Appeared as shadowy green skull. Nearly killed entire fellowship in epic battle. Soul shattered into dark emerald phylactery. Phylactery later batted into ocean by Shorea, presumed destroyed by salt water.

**Current Status:** DESTROYED, phylactery in ocean

### The Red Horde

#### Gauorezz Nightstalker
**Hobgoblin Lieutenant**

Ugluk's hobgoblin lieutenant. Direct orders found signed with red hand signature. Commands hobgoblin commando units recruiting forest orc tribes. Current location unknown.

**Current Status:** At large, commanding Horde units

#### Tauren Rockbreaker
**Giant King**

Giant king who led siege assault that broke the Gray Gate. Commands siege giants and war engines. Overwhelming force that devastated the fortress.

**Current Status:** Commanding giant forces in Gray Wastes

### House Aetherius

#### Lord Tartulien
**Baron | War Criminal**

Aetherius baron who massacred Ulrick's village and family, including his sister. Ulrick's primary target for personal revenge. Appeared at Council of the Wood representing Aetherius, insulted fallen Commander Sapentus. Shorea cried out in solidarity for Sapentus, drawing crowd support.

**Current Status:** ALIVE, Ulrick seeks revenge

#### Lord Garrulien
**Mage Noble**

Rarely seen lord of Tower of Dusk. Operates or connected to The Inquisition secret police. Mysterious figure controlling Aetherius intelligence.

**Current Status:** At Tower of Dusk

### The Feywild

#### Lord Irellion
**Fey Noble | Black Armor**

Fey noble serving Vissigeron the Cruel. Appeared through shimmering green portal in tree. Attempted to recruit Ulrick to come to Feywild. Charmed Arca and Shorea. Spoke in riddles about "rebalancing" the forest through the blight. Disappeared when fellowship refused his offer.

**Current Status:** Serving Vissigeron, opposed to fellowship

#### Oberon
**Fey King | Ulrick's Patron**

King of the Feywild, son of mad fey king Nelos who broke ancient fey-elf alliance. Made pact with Ulrick for warlock powers. Gives missions to weaken Nejune and cede territory to nature. Challenged Ulrick to single combat - Ulrick scored one hit. Named Ulrick his Champion. Seeks permanent portal to Material Plane and alliance with Fellwood.

**Current Status:** Ruling Feywild, using Ulrick as agent

### Dragons

#### Vissigeron the Cruel
**Elder Green Dragon**

Ancient foe of the Fellwood. Lair unknown. Feared by Aenarion and Fayrus. Lord Irellion serves him. Desires the blight to "rebalance" forest and reclaim as his domain. Has truce with Fey Court (Oberon warns Ulrick he's off limits).

**Current Status:** At large, scheming in shadows

#### Nerongnar
**Black Dragon | DECEASED**

Lived under Draconiss temple and dojo. Received gifts from local orc, kobold, and Golden Order tribes. Slayed by fellowship. Vast horde recovered including Master Goro's legendary blade.

**Current Status:** DEAD, horde looted

#### Beornn the White Death
**Ancient White Dragon | DECEASED**

Lived atop frozen peaks of Gray Mountains. Hunted by Lady Mera (she survived, most of her team died). Fellowship slayed him narrowly in mid-air battle. Ulrick used polymorph and feather token. Tooth required for Oberon's mission.

**Current Status:** DEAD, tooth taken, horde looted

#### Tiamat
**Goddess of Evil Dragons | WOUNDED**

Nemesis of Bahamut. Shorea and REN combined power to slay her in spiritual vision at Temple of Bahamut, banishing wind of chaos from the temple. This may have weakened her significantly in the Material Plane.

**Current Status:** Banished from temple, wind of chaos removed

### The Wardens (Destroyed)

Criminal organization in Dargo underworld building Outer Realm gateway. Entire organization destroyed when fellowship and Dargo forces assaulted their lair. Grey Scar slain, gateway destroyed (though one alien hulk and severed webbed arm made it through before closure).

**Current Status:** DESTROYED as organization

### The Circle of the Moon

Mysterious druid circle believed to be behind the Blightrot according to rumors. May have released Neerius from second sealed gate. True extent of their involvement unknown.

**Current Status:** Unknown, possibly conspiracy theory

## Historical Figures

### The Companions of Lokhir

Ancient heroes who served with the god Lokhir in the founding of Dargo. Their tombs lie beneath Earl Carth's manor:
- One raised as death knight by Lathian (freed from curse by fellowship)
- Three others entombed with relics
- Sacred burial site defiled by vampire

### Saint Bel'raa
**Oracle of Alaya | DECEASED**

Ancient apostle of Alaya. Her tomb was cursed by Maldrak the Blackened for centuries. Soul trapped until fellowship broke the curse. Ascended to join Alaya after thanking the heroes. Left behind her amulet of antimagic and other apostles' artifacts.

## The Forsaken

Survivors of the Tower of Dawn corruption. Insane and mutated by exposure to Outer Realm energies. Stream into Dargo (taken to Temple of Lokhir) and Aetherius (rounded up in concentration camps). Non-Euclidean geometry and time distortion affected their minds and bodies permanently.
