#### Individual Kingdom Pages (8)
All with complete stats, government details, military power, economy, religion, relationships, notable NPCs:

1. **`kingdoms/volanlek.md`** - Vol'Anlek (High Elves, B Tier)
   - Two noble houses: Alephor (religious/magical) and Horun (military)
   - Golden Legion details
   - Red Crusade history and Archknight Maldrake
   - Complete NPC profiles: Striker Horun (S), Ludus Alephor (S)

2. **`kingdoms/tamaryen.md`** - Tamaryen (Yuan-Ti, A Tier)
   - Red/Black caste system
   - Black Order vs. Trinitum power struggle
   - Largest military force on continent
   - Complete NPCs: Praetor Sasaren (A), Lord Zaz Crimsonscale (A)

3. **`kingdoms/ironhelm.md`** - Ironhelm (Dwarves, S Tier)
   - Imperial confederacy of clans
   - Gunpowder invention
   - Orc extermination and Duergar war
   - Complete NPCs: Emperor Aldrick Ironshield (S), King Huren Dragonhelm (B)

4. **`kingdoms/dragmar-aer.md`** - Dragmar Aer (Aarakocra, B Tier)
   - Plutocratic matriarchy
   - Djinn alliances
   - Ket's death and religious crisis
   - Complete NPC: Queen Rerumaj Cloudfeather (B)

5. **`kingdoms/ithiltara.md`** - Ithiltara (Sea Elves/Gnomes, C Tier)
   - Vassal under Dragmar Aer
   - Free trade port
   - Explorer's Association headquarters
   - Complete NPC: Seamaster Laedaere (B)

6. **`kingdoms/cormandir.md`** - Cormandir (Mixed, B Tier)
   - Aristocratic magocracy
   - The Magisterium
   - Only independent power after Red Crusade
   - Complete NPC: Grand Magister Tenebros (S+)

7. **`kingdoms/jalurian.md`** - Jalurian (Half-Orc/Human, C Tier)
   - Protectorate under Tamaryen
   - Haven for exiles and pirates
   - Orc genocide survivors
   - Complete NPC: Warchief Ka Orum (C)

8. **`kingdoms/saint-helensberg.md`** - Saint Helensberg (Mixed, D Tier)
   - Vassal under Vol'Anlek
   - Most religious kingdom
   - Democratic governance
   - Complete NPC: Lord Gelus Greyhawk (C)
